Functional Programming Day

25 May 2019 - Paris

A day long open space dedicated to everything functional programming: Languages, platforms, tools, techniques...

A full day to discover, exchange and practice a modern and yet decades old approach to software development. Where practitioners meets theorists, hobbyists meet professionals, newbies meet oldies.

Register

Fostering Exchange in Diversity


Functional paradigm has become pervasive within all platforms, languages, architectures. All mainstream languages now boast to support some form of functional languages features, be it lambdas, immutable data structures, reactive streams, composable abstractions. And thanks to advances in compilers and virtual machines, efficiency in program using higher-order functional abstractions is no more a major concern whichever architecture one targets.

fpday wants to be a Community Driven event for all functional paradigm enthusiasts, to foster exchange across diverse communities and to spread knowledge and awareness of this paradigm to newcomers. It is tailored to allow each participant to bring and takeaway something: A problem, a solution, a savoir-faire, a useful library or framework, a dream...
